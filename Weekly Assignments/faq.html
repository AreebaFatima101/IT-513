Frequently Asked Questions

What courses are offered?

We offer a wide range of courses including Web Development, Graphic Design, and Data Science.

How can I apply?

You can apply online through our admissions portal by filling out the registration form.

Is financial aid available?

Yes, scholarships and need-based financial aid options are available for eligible students.

Where is the campus located?

Our main campus is located in Faisalabad, near the city center with easy public transport access.

Can I take classes online?

Yes, most of our programs are available in both online and hybrid learning formats.
